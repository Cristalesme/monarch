Transportation

The Bus is a cheap and reliable option that is accessible to almost anywhere. Schedules can be viewed online and there are apps like Moovit that give live times for any bus/rail link you need.
--- LA https://www.metro.net/riding/maps/
--- OC https://www.octa.net/Bus/Routes-and-Schedules/Overview/
If you have friends or family that might take a similar route from you, ask them to drop you off or you can offer money as well in exchange.
If you are taking a long trip or just want to be able to drive yourself, consider renting a car.
--- https://www.immihelp.com/newcomer/car-rental-tips.html
Another alternative to traveling long distances is taking the train to your destination.
--- CA Metrolink https://www.metrolinktrains.com/schedules/
In California, Colorado, Connecticut, Delaware, Hawaii, Illinois, Maryland, New Mexico, Nevada, Utah, Vermont, and Washington, it is possible to get a driver's’ license without being a US citizen.
--- https://www.dmv.org/ca-california/ab-60-drivers-license.php
